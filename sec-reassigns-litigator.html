SEC Reassigns Top Litigator Amid Crypto Legal Battles

The Securities and Exchange Commission has reassigned Jorge Tenreiro, its top litigator involved in significant lawsuits against crypto exchanges, to an administrative role. This move signals a potential shift in the SEC's regulatory approach towards the crypto market.
